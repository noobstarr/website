About Miscellania-PS

We are a 317 based RSPS with no customs and no OSRS content. Our game and cache is modeled after the mid-2005 version of RS, we WILL NOT be adding any kind of content passed this era and intend to keep it that way. However there are and will be new bosses added and new content added often, you can also count on plenty of community based events. There are several quests and more being added, the experience rate is x6 and the shops are very limited.
